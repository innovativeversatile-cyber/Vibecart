Privacy overview

This page summarizes how VibeCart approaches privacy and data protection. It is a working overview and should be reviewed by a qualified lawyer before you rely on it as a full Privacy Policy in production.

What we may collect

  • Account and profile information you choose to provide (for example email, name, and country).
  • Order, listing, and messaging data needed to operate the marketplace and prevent fraud.
  • Technical data such as IP address, device type, and approximate location derived from network signals.
  • Support and compliance records when you contact us or when we investigate abuse.

Why we use data

  • To provide marketplace services, payments-related workflows where enabled, and delivery coordination.
  • To detect fraud, enforce platform rules, and protect buyers and sellers.
  • To improve security, reliability, and product experience.
  • To meet legal obligations where applicable.

Retention and sharing

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes above, dispute resolution, and legal compliance. We may use trusted service providers (for example hosting, email delivery, and payment processors) under contracts that require appropriate safeguards. We do not sell personal data.

Your choices

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion where applicable law allows.
  • You may opt out of non-essential communications where offered.
  • You may contact us using the address below for privacy-related requests.
