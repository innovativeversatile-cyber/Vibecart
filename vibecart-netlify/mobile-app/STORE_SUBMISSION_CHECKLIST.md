# Store Submission Checklist

## Android (Google Play)

- Create Google Play Console account.
- Set package name to `com.vibecart.mobile`.
- Provide:
  - app icon and feature graphic
  - screenshots (phone + tablet if supported)
  - short + full descriptions
- Complete Data Safety form (data collection, encryption, sharing).
- Upload signed AAB build from EAS.
- Add Privacy Policy URL and Terms URL.
- Declare permissions justification (especially notifications).
- Pass Play pre-launch report and policy checks.

## iOS (App Store)

- Create Apple Developer account.
- Confirm bundle ID `com.vibecart.mobile`.
- Provide:
  - app name, subtitle, categories
  - screenshots by required device sizes
  - app privacy details
- Add Privacy Policy URL and support URL.
- Upload archive via EAS/Xcode workflow.
- Complete export compliance and content rights forms.
- Pass App Review guidelines for commerce and user-generated content.

## Legal + Compliance Required

- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Acceptable Use / Prohibited Items policy
- Refund/Return policy
- Regional legal notices and contact address

## Security Verification Before Submit

- HTTPS only for all API/web content.
- Owner auth backend live with MFA.
- Fraud and abuse detection enabled.
- Moderation/reporting flow active.
- Crash/error monitoring enabled.
