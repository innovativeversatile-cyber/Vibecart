Restore coach access (no second charge)

If payment succeeded but you saw an error screen instead of your plan workspace, your purchase is still valid. Paste the Checkout session ID from your Stripe receipt (starts with cs_) — not your email address. We verify it with Stripe and open your coach workspace. You are not charged again.

You must be signed in with the same VibeCart account (and email) you used at checkout. If anything was clipped when copying, open the Stripe receipt in your browser and copy the part after session_id= in the address bar. You can paste a full Stripe URL here — we pull the ID out automatically.
