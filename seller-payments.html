Seller payments & payouts

VibeCart uses escrow-style settlement on live orders: buyer pays in, funds release after both sides confirm delivery. For marketplace listings, your shop must be active and policy-compliant before payouts run.

Stripe & Connect

When Stripe Connect is enabled on the server, sellers complete a short onboarding flow so payouts can land in your bank. Until then, orders may still be created but payout release can stay queued — check Orders & tracking after each sale.

Fees you should expect

Platform service fees and shipping quotes are shown at checkout where the API is wired. Keep your listing prices inclusive of what you are willing to absorb.

Tax & compliance

You remain responsible for VAT / sales tax and income reporting in your country. Read the policy & lawful-use page and keep origin and shipping text accurate.
