# VibeCart Revenue Playbook

## Goal

Build predictable monthly revenue from:
- cross-border product orders
- service bookings
- seller growth tools
- brand advertising
- logistics margin
- affiliate conversions

## Recommended pricing ranges

Use these as launch defaults, then optimize quarterly.

### 1) Marketplace take rate (orders)

- Starter sellers: `9%` + fixed `0.20` local-currency equivalent
- Pro sellers: `7%`
- Premium sellers: `5.5%`

Country-route guidance:
- Poland to Southern/East Africa: +`1.0%` convenience premium (for FX and risk controls)
- Intra-country transactions: reduce by `0.5%` for growth
- High-return categories: +`0.5%` risk buffer

### 2) Seller subscriptions

- `Starter`: 9.99 EUR/month (small catalog + 2 boost credits)
- `Pro`: 29.99 EUR/month (larger catalog + analytics + priority support)
- `Premium`: 79.99 EUR/month (unlimited catalog, bigger boost bundle, fastest payouts)

Localization rule:
- Display local equivalent currency price by market while settling in your base accounting currency.

### 3) Listing boosts and featured placement

- 3-day search top: 12 EUR
- 7-day home feed: 25 EUR
- 14-day category featured: 40 EUR
- Service spotlight weekend pack: 15 EUR

Peak multipliers:
- Back-to-school, Black Friday, festive: `x1.25` to `x1.6`

### 4) Buyer paid add-ons

- Buyer protection plan: 1.5% to 2.5% of cart value
- Fast checkout + currency shield: fixed 1.00 to 2.00 local equivalent
- Priority dispute review: fixed 3.00 to 6.00 local equivalent

### 5) Service booking monetization

- Booking commission: `8%` baseline
- Verified provider badge: 4.99 EUR/month
- Last-minute slot boost: fixed 2.00 per promoted slot

### 6) Advertising monetization

- Sponsored search keyword packs
- Sponsored category cards
- Geo-targeted campus bundles

Suggested minimum ad invoice:
- 200 EUR/month equivalent per campaign

### 7) Logistics margin

Route-level spread model:
- `platform_price - provider_cost = margin`
- Keep margin between 8% and 20% by route reliability and demand

### 8) Affiliate revenue

Partners:
- student wallets
- telcos/data bundles
- insurance
- shipping supplies

Commission target:
- 4% to 12% per conversion category

## Clientele-focused monetization strategy

For your main user segments:

- Students: low fixed fees, bundles, campus ambassador affiliate codes
- Cross-border families/resellers: transparent FX + protection plans
- Beauty/service providers: subscription + slot boosts + lower take rate at volume
- Brands/advertisers: geo-targeted packages by country and category

## Revenue KPIs to track weekly

- Gross merchandise value (GMV)
- Take-rate realized (%)
- Subscription MRR
- Boost attach rate (% of active sellers buying boosts)
- Protection-plan attach rate
- Ad fill rate and ad ARPU
- Logistics margin by route
- Affiliate conversion revenue

## Guardrails

- Always show fees before checkout confirmation
- Respect local consumer law on surcharge transparency
- Keep tax and payout ledger records immutable
- Add anti-fraud checks before enabling high-margin features
