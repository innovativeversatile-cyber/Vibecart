# Ad Monetization Model

## Goal

Allow legal brands and companies to pay for ad placements on VibeCart with minimal manual interaction.

## Self-Serve Flow

1. Advertiser registers and verifies legal identity.
2. Advertiser creates campaign budget and target regions.
3. Advertiser submits creative.
4. Platform moderation approves/rejects creative.
5. Approved ads are served in small sponsored slots.
6. Billing is calculated from fixed slot, CPM, or CPC rules.

## Safety Rules

- Only legal products and legal advertisers are accepted.
- All creatives must pass moderation.
- Ads must be marked as "Sponsored".
- Suspicious click/impression patterns should be flagged.

## Revenue Suggestions

- Starter campaign minimum: EUR 50.
- Small slot format to protect user experience.
- Initial model: fixed slot + optional CPC tracking.
- Keep ad share low to maintain conversion and trust.
